### How It Works

1. **Pick a Track** — Click a tab at the top
2. **Select a Module** — Use the sidebar to navigate
3. **Flip the Cards** — Click any card to reveal the answer
4. **Filter by Tag** — Use the tag cloud to find specific topics (e.g., "ROI & Value", "Competition")
5. **Take the Exam** — Pass 3 questions to earn your certification badge
